About Me

I am Rishi Ghia, a Computer Science Master’s student at the University of Pennsylvania, graduating in May, 2024. Over the last year I have honed my skills in Machine Learning and its applications such as Deep Learning, Natural Language Procession and Computer Vision. Through coursework, independent projects, internships, and research work, I have built a strong technical and practical foundation in these technologies, which is further complemented by my proficiency in several programming languages such as Python, Java, C, C++ and relevant libraries and tools such as TensorFlow and PyTorch.
