About Me

I am Rishi Ghia, a Computer Science Master’s student at the University of Pennsylvania, graduating in May, 2024. Over the last year I have honed my skills in Machine Learning and its applications such as Deep Learning, Natural Language Procession and Computer Vision. Through coursework, independent projects, internships, and research work, I have built a strong technical and practical foundation in these technologies, which is further complemented by my proficiency in several programming languages such as Python, Java, C, C++ and relevant libraries and tools such as TensorFlow and PyTorch. I am currently seeking new graduate roles for 2024 in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Machine Perception, and NLP, but my versatile skill set also makes me a strong candidate for Software Development roles.
